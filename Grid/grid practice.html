Welcome to our Jamia
After the migration to Pakistan, Maulana Mufti Mufti Muhammad Shafi Sahib, may Allah bless him and grant him peace, had made two tasks his goal in life. One is the struggle for the implementation of Islamic Shariat in Pakistan, the other is the establishment of the magnificent Darul Uloom in Karachi.
